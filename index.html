About Us

The Honda House is an ever-growing and changing one-stop reference for all things pertaining to vintage Honda motorcycles! If you notice any wrong or outdated information, please e-mail us!

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide a detailed and reliable database for all available information on vintage Honda Motorcycles. This includes specs, parts fiche, specs, and an updated fair market price. We strive to have the most accurate and up-to-date information!
